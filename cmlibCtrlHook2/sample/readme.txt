cmlibCtrlHook_sample

# sample.prx(KernelModule)
1. Swap Square / Select button.
2. Invalid of Circle button.
3. A push on a Circle button will repeal a Left & Right & Up & Down key.
4. A Left & Right & Up & Down key will be validated if a Circle button is pushed once again.

# sample2.prx(UserModule)
1. Swap Left / L_trigger & Right / R_trigger buttons.
2. A push on a Triangle button will repeal a Cross button.
3. A Cross button will be validated if a Triangle button is pushed once again.
